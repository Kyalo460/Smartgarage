Welcome to Smartgarage, where you can make appointments to get your car fixed.

Smart Garage is a website for booking garage appointments. You can book an appointment to either get your car fixed or to get maintenance done on your car. When registering, you get to provide your car's model; this is important because it let's us know the type of car we'll be working with. So be sure to provide the correct details when signing up.

An image of a car being fixed at our shop

If you don't have an account, you can start by signing up . Signing up is pretty simple, you just have to provide credentials like: first name, last name, phone number, car model, email and password.

After signing up, you'll be redirected to a login page where you can login by providing your username and password.

When you login, you'll be redirected to an appointments page where you can book appointments. To book an appointment, you will be required to give the details of your appointment and provide the date and time that you would like to come in.

About:

This is a portfolio project developed by Brian Kyalo and Caroline Kalumu .It was an assignment issued at ALX SE (an online school for Software Engineering). We were given the freedom to choose the project that we would do and technologies that we were going to use. I (Brian Kyalo) was studying node js and express js on the side, so I convinced my partner to use those languages for the backend. This meant that I would be the one tasked with coming up with the backend infrastracture. We were given three weeks to complete the project. I made the github repository and added my teammate as a collaborator and we scheduled google meetings for us to collaborate on the project. This project was an excitng journey filled with challenges and triumphs which led to us learning more about Software Engineering.
